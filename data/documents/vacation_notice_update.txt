Subject: Update to Minimum Notice Period for Employee Vacation Requests

1.  Purpose This document updates the existing internal directive
    governing employee vacation requests. The purpose of this update is
    to adjust the minimum notice periods required for submitting
    vacation requests.

2.  Scope This update applies to all employees of The Company,
    regardless of employment type or seniority.

3.  Policy Update Effective from the date specified above, all minimum
    notice periods for vacation requests are increased to double their
    previously defined durations.

Examples: - If the original minimum notice period was 2 days, it is now
4 days. - If the original minimum notice period was 1 week, it is now 2
weeks. - If the original minimum notice period was 1 month, it is now 2
months.

4.  Rationale This adjustment is implemented to improve workforce
    planning, ensure operational continuity, and allow for more
    effective resource allocation.

5.  Compliance All employees are required to comply with the updated
    notice periods. Managers are responsible for enforcing this
    directive.

6.  Transitional Provisions Vacation requests submitted prior to the
    effective date of this directive will be processed under the
    previously applicable rules.

7.  Final Provisions This document is issued for demonstration purposes
    only and does not represent a legally binding policy. The Company
    reserves the right to amend this directive at any time.
